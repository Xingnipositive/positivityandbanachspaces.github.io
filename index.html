Positivity and Banach spaces
July 1 to July 5, 2024
Chern Institute of Mathematics
Nankai University
Tianjin, P.R.China
CIM homepage

We are pleased to announce that the conference on “Positivity and Banach spaces” will be held at Chern Institute of Mathematics, Tianjin, P.R.China during 1-5, July 2024.

The aim of this conference is to bring together mathematicians, experts and young, that show up-to-date contributions in Positivity and Banach space theory and its connections to related areas like Banach lattices, operator theory, operator algebras, homological theory, harmonic analysis or geometry of Banach spaces.

Please note that, July 1st is arrival day and there will be no talks on this day. Formal talks are scheduled from July 2nd to July 4th (all day), and on the morning of July 5th.

We look forward to seeing you at Positivity and Banach spaces. For more information, please contact the organizing committee at positivity.and.banach.spaces@outlook.com

Traffic information

CIM Address: No. 94 Weijin Road, Nankai District, Tianjin, P.R.China

  1. From Beijing Capital Airport to CIM.

    For visitors arriving at Beijing Capital Airport, please take the airport shuttle bus (board at Gate 1 on the first floor of Terminal 3 or Gate 15 on the first floor of Terminal 2) to Tianjinxi Long-Distance Bus Station ( Timetable of the shuttle bus:Beijing Capital International Airport—Tianjinxi Long-Distance Bus Station). After you arrived Tianjinxi Long-Distance Bus Station, you could take a taxi to Nankai University or follow the direction No.4.

    If you prefer to take the high-speed rail, you can take the airport shuttle or subway to Beijing South Railway Station after arriving at Beijing Capital International Airport. And then transfer to Tianjin Railway Station, Tianjinnan Railway Station or Tianjinxi Railway Station by high-speed rail. From Tianjin railway stations to Nankai University, please go to direction No.3 and No.4.
