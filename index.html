CIM homepage

We are pleased to announce that the conference on “Positivity and Banach spaces” will be held at Chern Institute of Mathematics, Tianjin, P.R.China during 1-5, July 2024.

The conference is dedicated to recent developments in Positivity and Banach space theory in their broadest senses, and to those on the interface between these fields. The goal of the conference is to bring together experts and younger researchers to discuss the current and future directions of these developments.

Please note that June 30 is the arrival day and that there will be no talks on this day. Formal talks are scheduled from July 1 to July 4 (all day), and on the morning of July 5.

We look forward to seeing you at "Positivity and Banach spaces". For more information, please contact the organizing committee at positivity.and.banach.spaces@outlook.com

    For visitors arriving at Beijing Capital Airport, please take the airport shuttle bus (board at Gate 1 on the first floor of Terminal 3 or Gate 15 on the first floor of Terminal 2) to Tianjinxi Long-Distance Bus Station ( Timetable of the shuttle bus:Beijing Capital International Airport—Tianjinxi Long-Distance Bus Station). After you arrived Tianjinxi Long-Distance Bus Station, you could take a taxi to Nankai University or follow the direction No.4.

And then transfer to Tianjin Railway Station, Tianjin South Railway Station or Tianjin West Railway Station by high-speed rail. From Tianjin railway stations to Nankai University, please go to direction No.3 and No.4.

  2. From Tianjin Binhai International Airport（TSN）to CIM.

  3. From Tianjin Railway Station to CIM.

    For visitors arriving at Tianjin Railway Station, please take metro line 3 (bound for Tianjin South Railway Station) to Tianta Station and walk to Nankai University, or take bus No. 8/No. 832 and get off at Balitai Station (and then walk to the east gate of Nankai University).

  5. From Beijing Daxing International Airport（PKX） to CIM.

    If you land at Daxing Airport and want to travel to the Tianjin area, you will have 3 modes of transportation to choose from: taxi, bus and train. Although taking a taxi from Daxing Airport can be comfortable, the fare can be very high if traffic is heavy. In comparison, although China's trains are very fast, there is currently no dedicated line connecting Tianjin to the new airport. Therefore, you need to transfer to 4 high-speed trains and subways, and the journey will be tiring and time-consuming. Finally, take the intercity airport bus and you will reach your destination in approximately 2.5 hours.

    Taxi. Currently, Daxing Airport taxis do not offer fixed prices. However, a rough estimate of the fare for the approximately 130km journey from Daxing Airport to Tianjin city center is around 360 yuan (€46/$50), depending on actual traffic conditions and the exact drop-off location. In addition, at night, an additional 20% of the fare is required. If traffic conditions are good, it takes about 90 minutes to drive to the center of Tianjin.

    Bus. Taking an intercity bus from Daxing Airport to Tianjin is another transportation option. The entire trip takes about 2.5 hours, and you can either go to Tianjin Tongsha Long-distance Bus Station (the city's main bus station), or go to the Tianjin West Railway Station bus station near Tianjin West Railway Station. A one-way ticket costs 75 yuan (9.5 euros/10.5 USD). After you arrived the Bus Stations, you could take a taxi to Nankai University or follow the direction No.4.
